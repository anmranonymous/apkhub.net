Yes! Every single APK is scanned by our AI-powered security engine through 47 unique checks before being listed. We also manually review each file. Our 99.98% clean record proves our commitment to your safety.

Is APK Hub Pro completely free?▼

Absolutely. APK Hub Pro is 100% free and will always remain free. We are funded by optional voluntary donations and do not display intrusive advertising or require any registration.

How often are apps updated?▼

Our team monitors major app updates daily. New versions are typically available on APK Hub Pro within 12–48 hours of the official release. Subscribe to our Telegram for instant update alerts.

Why do I need to enable "Unknown Sources"?▼

Android's built-in security restricts installation of apps from outside the Google Play Store by default. To install any APK file, you must allow installations from unknown sources in your Android Settings → Security. This is completely normal and safe when using trusted sources like us.

How do I request a new app?▼

Simply use the contact form above or message us on Telegram with the app name and Play Store link. Our team evaluates all requests and tries to add highly-requested apps within 3–5 working days.
